Paris

Paris

In Paris you will be staying at the 4-star Hotel le Sénat close to the Luxembourg Gardens. On the night of the show, you will enjoy a 3-course meal at the intimate restaurant L’Épidon which serves outstanding organic food. The show will be at the grand Odéon Theatre, which is one of France’s national theatres.
Get in touch with us to find out which shows are available while you’re there!

London

London

In London you will be staying at the luxurious 5-star Courthouse hotel which offers an indoor pool and a spa. On the night of the show you will enjoy a 3-course meal at the bright and elegant Mews of Mayfair Brasserie. The show will be at the famous West End theatre London Palladium where you can pick between concerts, musicals and plays.
Get in touch with us to find out which shows are available while you’re there!

Prague

Prague

In Prague you will be staying at the central and quiet 4-star Hotel Leonardo which is close to the Charles Bridge. On the night of the show you will enjoy a 3-course meal at the cosy Restaurant Parnas where you will be able to look out over the Vitava River while dining. The show will be at the magnificent National Theatre where you can pick between ballet, operas and plays.
